Our Service!

How to utilize our product to make your life better


This service will allow you to estimate the volume of paint that you will need to complete your painting jobs. This may be used by anyone who has a need! We intended this to be utilized by both professionals and amateurs alike. We hope that this service will help you to save time and money by reducing the amount of paint that you need to purchase.


To use this service, you will need to create an account. This will allow you to save your calculations and access them at a later date. You will be able to access your projects from any device that has internet access. You will also be able to access your projects from any browser that you choose to use. This service is designed to be used on a desktop computer, but it will work on mobile devices as well.


First, you must make an account. Next, you can select the "Calculate Paint Neede" button in the menu bar to start your first calculation. On this page, you will be able to enter the dimensions of the room that you are trying to paint. We accomodate the inclusion of window and door values as well so that you can exclude their area from the calculation if you choose. We are proud to announce that you can choose between US standard units or the metric system. Once you have entered the dimensions of the room, you can press the "Calculate" button to see the results of your calculation. The volume of paint that you will need will is stored in your profile information, allowing you to calculate the paint required to paint multiple rooms all as part of a single project. You can find this total volume by selecting the "Profile" button in the menu bar. In your Profile page, you will be able to see the sum total of paint that you will need to complete all of your projects. Once you have finished your project, you can reset your calculated volume to 0 so that you can use this service again for future projects.
